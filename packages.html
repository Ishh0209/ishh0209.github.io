Travel Information

Planning

Our waistlist is long, as we are one of the only space tourism companies. To meet everypone's demands we hope that you can be patient with us. We will try to do everything to make this a wonderful expierence for you. In order to book the earlist slot make sure to provide all the correct information as well as proof of yout Space Visa. The entire trip from training to recovery will take 2 months. Please note this can be longer if your body takes longer to adjust when you land.

Training

We have a 2 week long training camp that all travellers must go thorugh. During this time you are put to test in simulated environment to show you what you will feel like in space. You are also put in clothing and suits that you would wear daily in space. If you do not adapt in this 2 week time period we recommend that you do not continue with this trip due to health risks.

Vechiles

Our vechiles get designed by some of the best engineers to meet our high standards. We have long testing periods for each vechile to ensure that they are safe for our passengers. The vechile you will board on will depend on how mnay people we have flying out that day. The two vechiles are not too different from one another, so the experience is similar in both. We try to be energy efficent when we have a smaller group, which is why we have two different vechiles. In our vechiles you will feel just like you are at home due to our advanced technology that adjusts the environment inside the ship or plane to be the same as on earth.

Launch Process

The launching process is similar to taking off in a flight, except it is longer and you will be wearing your suits. It takes around 17 hours to get to space and we encourage that you do not move out of your seat unless it is an emergency.

Recovery

Most astronauts take around 45 days to recover from being in space(Wayne). Due to our high technology systems our passengers only need 15-30 days to recover. However, there may be excepts if you have other health risks. During this period you are doing the opposite of what you did in training. Slowly every day we turn the space simulation down until you are back to earth's environment. You are also encourages to do physical activity every day, as being in space changes the way you move around in a normal setting.
